Why it matters
Last open port

Mobile's closure in August 1864 cut the Confederacy off from Havana and helped seal Lincoln's reelection.

Last major naval battle

“Damn the torpedoes” — Farragut's run through the minefield remains the defining image of Civil War naval combat.

Last major land battle

Fort Blakeley fell six hours after Lee surrendered at Appomattox — the men who charged that afternoon didn't yet know the war was over.

A county's long memory

The same ground the Historical Society documented is the same ground these battles were fought on.
